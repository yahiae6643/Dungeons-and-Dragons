RPG Club

“A break from the real world”


RPG Club is a small group where students can use their imaginations and storytelling in the world of Dungeons and Dragons. In D&D, the members create their own characters and are placed into a journey where they deal with great challenges.

Well that sounds cool, but how do I play?


There are some things needed to successfully create a character:

To start, you need to decide on your character’s combat class.

This determines how you will act in battle.

You also need to calculate the character’s stats.

The stats determine your strengths and weaknesses

The dice are also a very important part of D&D.

If you don’t have any of your own, some will be provided at RPG Club.

Think of your character’s personality!

This is really open ended, but remember that this is a team-based game. Your character should help everyone at the table!

You also need to think about the physical traits of the character. This includes weight, height, and race!
